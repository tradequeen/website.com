ABOUT US

TRADEBRANDS Import and Distribution Zambia Ltd is a leading wholesale distributor of international brands selling merchandise at substantially lower prices than are typically found at conventional retail/wholesale sources.

We specialise in a range of products including: Automotive PCR, TBR, and HGV Tyres, Automotive Batteries, Residential and Commercial Air Conditioners, Electrical and Mechanical Water Pumps and Reticulation Systems, Lighting, and Hospitality Supplies.

TRADEBRANDS head office and showroom is strategically located 10 minutes south of Lusaka with easy access to all major road networks for inward and outwards stock movement. We also have a large warehouse facility, which is able to hold sufficient stocks. It is important to note we also have an impressive fleet of vehicles to deliver this stock. With this in mind, we are able to deliver countrywide effortlessly.

OUR MISSION

is to represent our international brands responsibly and honestly in order to exceed their expectations, as well as our customers.

OUR GOALS

Our goal is to provide our customers with unbeatable trade prices on quality brands. Doing so will achieve continued annual profitability that will build a sustainable company for future generations.
